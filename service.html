FAQ

Any Questions? Look Here

Our rental process is straightforward: simply contact us to discuss your mission requirements, and we will match you with a suitable canine partner, ensuring all necessary training and documentation are in place.

We utilize a variety of breeds known for their intelligence, strength, and loyalty, including German Shepherds, Belgian Malinois, Dutch Shepherds, and Labradors.

The training process can vary depending on the specific dog and the desired skillset. However, on average, it takes several months of intensive training to prepare a dog for military operations.

Our dogs are trained by experienced handlers to excel in obedience, protection, and tactical support, making them mission-ready for high-stakes environments. Each dog is equipped with resilience, discipline, and adaptability, setting them apart from traditional service dogs and ensuring they’re prepared to meet the unique demands of their handlers.
